ACTIVITIES

Search Retreat Director

At the University of San Diego, I had the privilege to participate in assisting and leading the Search Retreat, which is a kairos model retreat that is for Sophomores through Seniors. It offers them a chance to recuperate from the stress of school and allow them to rest in the presence of their classmates and God. For this retreat, I have served as a behind the scenes role. My main responsibilities were to make posters for the retreat site, decorations, as well as organize files for each participant and help make specialized items such as candles and crosses for each person. I learned the importance of time management as we would have meetings for the retreat over the weekend amid the stress of school. It was key to ensure I stayed on top of my assignments and extracurricular activities. As a retreat leader, we stress the importance of servant leadership, which is the giving of yourself for the betterment of others, and I think this is a quality that will allow me to grow and prosper in any workplace, and in life.
